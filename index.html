OUR MENU

Sushi




Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with sugar and salt, a variety of ingredients, such as seafood and raw vegetables. Styles of sushi and its presentation vary widely.

Beef




Beef, flesh of mature cattle, as distinguished from veal, the flesh of calves. The best beef is obtained from early maturing, special beef breeds. High-quality beef has firm, velvety, fine-grained lean and well-marbled.

Chicken




Chicken is the most common poultry in the world. Owing to the relative ease and low cost of raising them have become prevalent throughout the cuisine of cultures around the world.
